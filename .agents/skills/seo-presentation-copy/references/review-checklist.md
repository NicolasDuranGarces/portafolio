# Review Checklist

Use this checklist when reviewing or rewriting homepage or landing page presentation copy.

## SEO

- Is the main entity named clearly?
- Is the main service or role named clearly?
- Is the main location included when useful?
- Is the primary query reflected in the headline or subheadline?
- Do headings and lead text support the same search intent?
- Are metadata, hero, and CTA language aligned?

## Ambiguity

- Does each sentence have a concrete subject?
- Are vague words replaced with observable outcomes?
- Are generic claims supported by stack, scope, audience, or result?
- Are there phrases that could mean several different things?

## Content Design

- Does the heading hierarchy scan well?
- Does the copy fit the component size?
- Are chips, labels, and card titles short enough?
- Do CTA labels say exactly what happens next?
- Is there any repeated text that adds noise instead of reinforcement?

## Positioning

- Is it clear who the page is for?
- Is it clear what problem is solved?
- Is it clear why this person or company is different?
- Is the tone credible for the audience?

## Rewrite Rules

- Compress first, expand only if the layout has room.
- Put the strongest phrase first.
- Use one main angle per section.
- Keep the page semantically consistent across hero, about, services, FAQ, and contact.
- Prefer concrete wording that could survive without visual design support.
